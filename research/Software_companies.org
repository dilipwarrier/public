* Open source software foundations

** Mozilla Foundation

They govern Firefox and Thunderbird development.

** Apache Foundation

They govern Apache Spark and Apache Kafka, among other projects. Their
focus is usually on data processing and analytics.

See [[id:be4c0342-3c8d-49ac-8046-f36b65361d72][Apache projects]].

** Eclipse Foundation
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       30978d6c-a649-4c4e-a4c1-3a124ca965e0
:END:

They govern the RTOS Eclipse ThreadX and the [[id:c1eb170e-2302-42ee-81fb-dadecba14839][Eclipse IDE]], among
other projects.

See also [[id:59b8b305-3e77-403e-99d1-51d6eb2f4297][Eclipse ThreadX]].
